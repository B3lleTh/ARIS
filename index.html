Our Identity

ARIS (Academic Research Index For Students) is a Project that aims to connect the two extremes of academic rigor and professional transition. Our platform uses trustworthy data obtained from the success metrics and career paths of graduate students. This experiential intelligence is classified and studied to offer actual instances on educational routes and best professional tactics. Our aim is to be a clear, utility-oriented, and reliable online space where all users can get the data-informed insights needed to make the best learning decisions and future career planning.
